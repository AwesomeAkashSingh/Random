💡 From BITS Pilani's AI/ML Perspective

Your Sem 1 gave you the mathematical and algorithmic bedrock of AI. In Sem 2, you specialise. The field is bifurcating: language/NLP (GPT, LLMs, RAG) and vision/multimodal (autonomous vehicles, medical imaging) are the two dominant job markets. Deep Learning research (diffusion models, generative AI) requires UDL fundamentals. MLOps and data engineering roles need SEML and DM4ML.

Industry trend (2025–26): NLP + IR is the backbone of enterprise RAG pipelines. CV + VA powers robotics and surveillance. UDL is the research gateway to understanding how ChatGPT-style models are built from scratch.
